Lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious Italian meal that is great for any occasion!

Whether you are trying to impress your friends, or just looking for comfort food, this is the meal for you.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 bunch fresh spinach
  • 1 container ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
